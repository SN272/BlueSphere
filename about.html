About Us

FloatChat is an AI-powered conversational interface that allows researchers, policymakers, and communities to query, explore, and visualize ARGO float ocean data in plain English. By combining Large Language Models (LLMs), vector databases, and geospatial dashboards, FloatChat democratizes access to complex oceanographic datasets.
