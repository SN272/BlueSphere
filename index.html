🌊 Welcome to BlueSphere

Making ocean intelligence conversational, visual, and inclusive. Dive deep into the world's oceans through interactive visualizations, real-time data insights, and powerful AI-driven conversational queries.

🧠 AI-Powered Chatbot

Ask natural language questions about oceanographic data and get instant answers. No technical knowledge needed.

📊 Interactive Visualizations

Explore ocean maps, float trajectories, and depth-time plots designed to help researchers and enthusiasts understand data at a glance.

⚠️ Disaster Alerts

Automatically detects cyclone, tsunami, and flooding risks from incoming data and provides urgent safety warnings.

🌍 Multi-language Support

Inclusive design with support for Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, and English to help a diverse audience explore data with ease.

🔎 Why BlueSphere Matters

BlueSphere bridges the gap between oceanographic data experts and non-experts. Our tool democratizes access to ARGO float data, allowing:

  • Researchers & scientists to get easy access to climate and marine data.
  • Policy makers to make informed decisions based on real-time data insights.
  • Students & educators to learn ocean science interactively through gamified content.
  • Fishermen & coastal communities to get proactive safety alerts in their local language.
